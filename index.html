THE TIMELINE IS FRACTURED

Step into a broken timeline where zombie outbreaks are appearing across history.

In this endless top-down survival game, you are deployed into unstable time zones to eliminate threats and recover valuable resources. Each run drops you into a different moment in time — your objective is simple: survive as long as possible.

Fight off waves of enemies, collect coins, and discover blueprints for powerful upgrades. Everything you gather can be brought back and used to improve your loadout for the next deployment.

The longer you survive, the more intense it gets. Stronger enemies, faster waves, and complete chaos.

There is no escape. No final level.

Just survive… as long as you can.
